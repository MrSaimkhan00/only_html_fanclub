The First Bugatti: Type 13

The Bugatti Type 13, also known as the "Brescia," was the first production model created by the French automaker Bugatti in 1910.

With its distinctive design and innovative engineering, the Type 13 laid the foundation for Bugatti's reputation for excellence in automotive performance and luxury.
